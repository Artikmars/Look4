1. What is the program for?

- Look4 helps you to exchange contacts with a person nearby who also has this program installed. Moreover, the Internet is not needed for this, so you can easily exchange phone numbers like in the subway, airplane, etc.

2. How does Look4 work?

- To find each other, one person must turn on the "Online" mode, and his phone can be found by others, even if the phone is in an inactive mode (for example, lies in a purse). The one who is looking for - simply clicks the "Search" button - and if you find yourself in his access zone (about 20 meters), then you will receive a notification about the request for contacts exchange. You either accept or not. If everything worked out and you confirm the request, a phone number of a person will be saved in your contacts screen.

3. How to communicate further?

- In the contact screen, when you click on a phone number, it will be copied to the clipboard. This will allow you to continue communication in any of the messengers. 

4. How can I change my photo or number?

- You need to go to the Profile screen in the settings and click on your profile photo to change it.

5. Why is the gradation of Man, Women, All needed?

- The application allows you to exchange both business contacts and be used for dating. In this case, you have the opportunity to filter those who can find you by gender.
